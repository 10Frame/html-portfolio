The top Three Movies That I Remember From My Childhood

The Lion King

A classic Disney film, The Lion King was perpetually playing in my childhood home.

Hercules

Another Disney film, I'm around 90% sure I wore the VHS tape out on this one.

James and the Giant Peach

I am not sure who made this one, but it was another constant in my rotation. It doesn't hurt that I share a name with the titular character.
